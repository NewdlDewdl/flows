Late night · ~48 min · ends in sleep

For flexibility + libido + sleep after. Hip-focused, parasympathetic ending, mag glycinate at the close.

For UTD piano-practice breaks, hotel rooms, anywhere with a carpet. Hands & wrists, cervical, hip warmth, external rotation, W-Sit first reading (internal hip rotation, Step 1 of 9), coda.

For libido + clarity + the open hours ahead. Hip bloom + HIIT cascade + cold splash. Skip if sleep-debt.
Anytime · ~20 min · ends parasympathetic
The Upper Recovery
For tight shoulders, neck, sleep-deprived days. Cervical + thoracic + scapular work, light hip reset, 4-7-8 close. Nap-friendly.
