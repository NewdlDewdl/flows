The Upper Recovery

A flow for the shoulders, the neck, and the sleep that didn't come
four movements · ~18 min · mat or carpet · ends parasympathetic
For the day you slept 4 hours and woke with shoulders that won't unclench. Cervical and thoracic tension is the upstream cause of "wired-but-tired" early-morning waking — when the upper body can't release, the diaphragm can't reach low, breath stays shallow, and sleep architecture collapses around 4 AM. This flow doesn't fix the sleep debt; it removes the somatic block that's keeping you from paying it down.
